About Club Hub

Our Mission

The BYUI Club Hub is your central resource for discovering, joining, and learning about societies at Brigham Young University–Idaho. Our site connects students with opportunities to collaborate, grow, and share knowledge across disciplines. This project is built and maintained by students in the Technical Teamwork class, combining technical skills with collaboration to serve the campus community.
